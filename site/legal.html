What the tool is // and is not

verify_pm_trade is an automated logic audit. It analyzes the reasoning in a trade thesis and returns a verdict computed by a deterministic engine. It is informational only. It is not financial, investment, trading, or legal advice; it does not predict outcomes; a PASS is not a recommendation to trade and does not mean a trade will be profitable. Verdicts can be wrong. Extraction can misread an intent (abstention exists for exactly this reason). You — or your agent — act entirely at your own risk, and you are responsible for your own decisions and their consequences.

Payments // x402, final, non-custodial

The audit costs 2.00 USDC per call on Base mainnet over x402. Payment is non-custodial: your EIP-3009 signature authorizes a transfer from your wallet directly to the DJZS treasury; the facilitator submits it and pays the gas. You sign the exact amount — nothing more can be taken. Every paid call is charged and payments are final, including calls that return in_scope: false, WAIT, FAIL, or an extraction failsafe: the charge pays for running the audit, not for a particular verdict. The free tools (query_pol_certificates, query_agent_trust) cost nothing.

Permanent public records // read before you submit

Every in-scope audit anchors a ProofOfLogic certificate to the Irys datachain — permanent and public, including extracted quotes from the intent you submitted. If you pass agent_address, the verdict is also written to a public on-chain trust record on Base. Neither can be edited or deleted by anyone, including us. Do not submit secrets, private keys, personal data, or anything you would not publish.

Privacy

No accounts, no logins, no cookies set by this site. Cloudflare serves the site and provides basic aggregate analytics (the beacon script). Submitted intents are processed to produce the audit — including LLM extraction — and survive only in the public certificate described above. Wallet addresses involved in payment or trust records are public on-chain data by nature.
